What to do on Taniti Island

Most people visit Taniti to enjoy the beaches, explore the rainforest, and to visit the volcano (See Taniti Island Page for details).

However, there are other things to do, including visiting several pubs, including a microbrewery, dancing at a new dance club, seeing a movie, playing at an arcade, visiting a local history museum, visiting art galleries, and bowling.

Also, a nine-hole golf course should be operational by next year.

Many of these activities are located in Merriton Landing, which is a rapidly developing area on the north side of Yellow Leaf Bay.
